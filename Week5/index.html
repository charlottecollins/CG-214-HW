Classy and Still Fun

As I was scrolling through the chrome experiment sites, I came across COLOR PIANO THEORY.

WHY IS THIS SITE SO AWESOME? (preface, you should enjoy classical music)

  1. It is secure without pop ups.
  2. There is just the right amount going on.
  3. You can choose to listen or go to player mode.
  4. There is a good selection of classical music.
  5. You can interact with the keyboard.
  6. And possibly learn something about piano.
  7. Rainbow.
